Our goal is to improve the environment

User is able to take a photo of the garbage and submit the photo with a label. Other users can judge on whether the label is correct. We introduced a new virtual currency “Green Coin”. User who labels the image uploaded by other users will earn green coins. User who labels their garbage correctly will earn credentials. The “Green Coin” can be used to pay the utility bills.
